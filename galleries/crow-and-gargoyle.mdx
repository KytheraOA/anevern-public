# Crow & Gargoyle 

"Crow & Gargoyle" are two original characters that began life as a series of silly sketches to cheer up a friend. As often happens with characters, they quickly took on a life of their own and grew far beyond their original purpose (which they served more than adequately). 

Crow (they/them) is an American crow (_Corvus brachyrhynchos_). The gargoyle is Gary (they/them), named because the autocorrect function of my friend's phone kept changing "garg" to "Gary." Gary has also become my mascot; something else I didn't originally plan, but it works!
