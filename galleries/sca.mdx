## Mirthlions 

The primary charge on the registered heraldry of the Barony of Lyondemere is a mer-lion (a lion with a fish tail instead of hind legs). I love drawing fantasy creatures, and I love drawing silly things (and puns). Thus the "mirthlion" was born.

## Pokkit Heraldree

These are trading card-sized renditions of heraldic devices. I began this project with the arms of the baronies and shires of the Kingdom of Caid, then expanded to draw people's personal registered heraldry.

## "Playing Card" Portraits

These trading card-sized portraits were commissioned by Queen Cassandra in [what year and AS?] during her reign with King Sven (which iteration?) as largesse to be gifted to the royalty or landed baronage that Their Majesties were visiting during their Royal Progress. The layout and clothing of the figures on the card are based on a [what era?] set of playing cards. Instead of card pips, I used the badge of the recipient's barony or kingdom. The colors and pattens on each figure's clothing is based on the registered heraldry of the recipient's barony or kingdom.
